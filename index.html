The Rivel Difference

Born from a vision of conscious luxury, Hotel Rivel stands as Costa Rica's premier non-profit retreat, where every stay contributes to local conservation and community empowerment.

Our Costa Rican founders have crafted an authentic sanctuary where modern luxury harmonizes with pristine nature, offering discerning travelers an transformative escape that nurtures both soul and environment.
